EXUBERENCE(group dance)
 Dance is a performance art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement.The rules for solo dance are designed in such a way that they donot favour any individual and make the competition fair.

RULES
  • Each team may have 8-12 members.
  • Choice of songs is open.
  • Time limit is 6-8minutes.Negative marking will be done for exceeding the time limit.
  • No props will be provided to the participants by the coordinators. Participant must bring their own props in any required.
  • Teams are requested to bring their sound track in 2 CDs and 1 pendrive to avoid any technical disturbances, out of which one CD should be submitted to the coordinator.
  • Performance should not convey any indecent gestures.
  • Green room will be provided.
  • Performance will be judged on the basis of their formations, synchronization, expressions, costumes , make up, choreography and overall effects.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.
  • Registration fee is Rs 500.
