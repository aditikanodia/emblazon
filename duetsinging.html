DUET SINGING
Singing is the act of producing musical sounds with the voice, and augments regular speech by the use of tonality, rhythm, the use of sustained tones and a variety of vocal techniques.Singers perform music that can be sung without accompaniment or with accompaniment by musical instruments.The rules for solo dance are designed in such a way that they donot favour any individual and make the competition fair.

RULES
  • Time allotted for each performance is 3-5 minutes.
  • heir will be two categories mainly Bollywood and Western.
  • Participants cannot refer to lyrics while singing.
  • The participants will be judged on the song selection ,voice quality ,feel ,clarity ,rhythm and synchronization .
  • Participants should bring their own musical instruments if required and they will have to bring their own karaoke file.
  • The deision of the judges will be final.
