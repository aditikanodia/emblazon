FACE PAINTING
 Dance is a performance art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement.The rules for solo dance are designed in such a way that they donot favour any individual and make the competition fair.

RULES
  • All participants must be registered on the day prior
  • All participants are responsible to be in the holding area prior to the start of program
  • Participants will be consider as a team (a team consisting of 2 contestants –one who will paint the face and other whose face is to be painted)
  • Use of unfair means to rules will result in immediate disqualification
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding as all participants
  • Time limit- 1hour
  • Bring your own materials
  • Only one prop is allowed
  • The artists will be given 1 minute to explain their work to the judges
  • Judging criteria-depiction of theme, finesse and explanation
