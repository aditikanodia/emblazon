SOLO DANCE
 Dance is a performance art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement.The rules for solo dance are designed in such a way that they donot favour any individual and make the competition fair.

RULES
  • There is no particular theme.
  • Choice of song is open to the participants.
  • The decision of judges will be final and bindind.
  • Participants should get there own props.
  • Time Limit: Minimum length of atleast 3 minutes and can extend to a maximum lenght of 4 minutes.Negative points for extending the time limit.
  • Participants are requested to bring 2 CDS to avoid technical disturbance.
  • Registration will be done on first come firstt serve basis(Registration fee Rs 150/-).
  • Participants will be judged on the basis of choreography, expression, costumes, make-up and overall effects.
