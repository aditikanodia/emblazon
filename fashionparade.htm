PANACHE-FASHION PARADE
 A fashion show is an event put on by a fashion designer to showcase his or her upcoming line of clothing during Fashion Week. Fashion shows debut every season, particularly the Spring/Summer and Fall/Winter seasons. This is where the latest fashion trends are made.At a fashion show, an audience sits and watches models in clothes from a designer's collection walk down a catwalk.

RULES
  • There should be 10-12 participants per team exclusive of the designer,choreographer and supporting members.
  • Time limit for the main round is 8-12 minutes(inclusive of stage setup).All teams should present their introduction before starting(extra 3-4 min will be given for that).Points will be deducted for exceeding the limits.
  • One person per team must be there to manage sound track and one for the lights.
  • Emphasis will be given to clothing,walking style and originality
  • No props and costumes will be provided by us.Teams have to bring their own costumes and props.
  • Use of materials like candles,matches,cigrattes,alcohol and any hazardous materials on stage is prohibited. Teams are required to contact organizers before using such stuffs.
  • Vulgarity of any kind would lead to disqualification of the team from the event.Hence,if the team feels that any stunt of costume design can be deemed as vulgar,it is strongly advised to consult organizers then performing it directly on stage.
  • The decision of the judges and organizing committe will be final and building in all.
  • Registration charges are free for our college's student.
  • It is imperative that all team members must possess a valid college ID which must be presented at the time of registration. However,designer and choreographer could be hired professional.
